Shawarma recipe

Shawarma

Shawarma is a Middle Eastern dish that originated in the Levant region of the Arab world during the Ottoman Empire, consisting of meat cut into thin slices, packed in a cone-like bread, and roasted on a slowly rotating vertical rotisserie or spit

Ingredients

  • Flat bread
  • Carrot
  • Chicken breast
  • Mayonnaise
  • Sausage
  • Spices
  • Ketchup
  • Onion
  • Cabbage

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken into sizes and soak it in seasoned mixture to taste
  2. Add 3 table spoons of groundnut oil to a pan and fry the chicken on low heat
  3. Add the sausage and keep stirring till itâ€™s fully cooked
  4. Cut the cabbage and carrot
  5. Mix the mayonnaise and ketchup together in a bowl and add some of it to the cabbage and carrot
  6. Get your flat bread, lay over it the cabbage and carrot mixture then the chicken and sausage and then finally the pepper sauce
  7. Scoop on the mayonnaise and ketchup on it and roll the wrap gently
  8. Put it on a shawarma pressing machine and press it for three to five minutes
  9. Serve with a very chilled drink
