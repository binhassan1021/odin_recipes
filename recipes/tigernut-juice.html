Tigernut juice

Ingredients

  • 5 cups Tiger Nuts
  • 15 dates
  • 1600 ml of water

Materiala

  • Bowls
  • Blender

Instructions

  1. You will need 5 cups of tiger nuts. If it is dried, you will have to soak it in a clean water for 24 hours before your blender could blend it
  2. If you are living outside Nigeria, possibilities are that you would only find the dry nuts. Soak them in plenty of water for 24 hours before attempting to turn them into tiger nut juice
  3. You will need a juice maker or a heavy duty blender, otherwise, it cannot blend it.
  4. Crack the dates to remove the seeds and blend together with the tiger nuts
  5. You can blend in batches if you are using a blender
  6. sieve using a siever or handkerchief
  7. Finally, your tiger nuts juice is ready
  8. Serve chilled
