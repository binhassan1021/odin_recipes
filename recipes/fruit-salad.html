Fruit-salad recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 red or green apples
  • 3 peeled tangerines or small oranges
  • 15 black or green grapes
  • 2 peeled medium bananas
  • 2 peeled kiwi fruits
  • 2 fresh large thick slices of pineapple
  • 2 glasses of fresh unsweetened orange juice - note: you can also use pineapple or apple juice

What you will need to prepare it

  • Chopping board
  • Cutting knife
  • Mixing bowl
  • Slotted spoon

Steps

  1. Wash the apples and then cut into quarters. Cut into large pieces
  2. Cut up the tangerines or oranges into segments
  3. Wash the grapes and cut into half each one
  4. Cut bananas and kiwi fruits into slices
  5. Cut pineapple into large pieces
  6. Pour all fruits in the bowl, add the orange juice and mix well

Serving suggestion

Serve on its own or with a scoop of ice cream or with block ice
